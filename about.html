关于我 / About Me

你好，我是李阳，一个喜欢在创意、策略与文化之间搭建桥梁的人。

Hi, I'm Yang — someone who thrives at the intersection of creativity, strategy, and cultural insight.

我的学习与工作经历横跨新闻、翻译与市场营销领域，一直对有深度、有温度的表达方式充满热情。

With a background spanning journalism, translation, and marketing, I've always been drawn to the power of meaningful communication.

跨文化的成长与实践让我拥有敏锐的洞察力与对差异的尊重，能够在不同观点与受众之间找到共鸣点。

My experiences across continents have shaped a sharp eye for detail and a deep respect for cultural nuance, helping me build bridges between ideas, people, and platforms.

我拥有昆士兰大学的新闻与口译双学士学位，以及墨尔本大学的管理学硕士（市场营销方向）。跨学科的训练使我在语言表达与战略思维方面具备扎实基础，也培养了我对品牌沟通更深层次的理解。

I hold a dual bachelor's degree in Journalism and Interpreting from the University of Queensland, and a Master of Management (Marketing) from the University of Melbourne. This cross-disciplinary background has given me a strong foundation in communication, with a focus on strategy, language, and brand expression.

我擅长将复杂的信息转化为有逻辑、有感染力的内容——不论是打造品牌语言，挖掘用户动机，还是将创意转化为可视化呈现。

I love turning complex insights into compelling narratives—whether it's shaping a brand's voice, exploring consumer motivations, or translating ideas into visuals.

我的思维方式融合了理性与直觉，既有逻辑支撑，也始终保持对未知的好奇和对意义的追求。

My approach blends analytical thinking with intuitive understanding, driven by curiosity and grounded in purpose.

在生活中，我重视清晰的沟通、真诚的协作与温和有力的态度。

Off the screen, I'm someone who values clarity, collaboration, and kindness.

我喜欢动植物、喜欢用双手做些温柔的东西，也喜欢讲故事。在快节奏之外，我更享受慢下来认真做一件小事的过程。

I love animals and plants, crafting by hand, and telling stories. I often slow down to think, and I enjoy immersing myself in one thing at a time.

我相信，真诚是人与人之间最好的起点，而持续学习与创造，是我通往世界的方式。

I believe that honesty is the start of all connection, and that learning and creating are the ways I choose to engage with the world.
