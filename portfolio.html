和乐怡品牌传播策划案 Horoyoi Brand Campaign
奥美广告 Ogilvy | 2023年春 Spring 2023

项目背景 / Background

面对中国低度酒"悦己消费"风口，Horoyoi（和乐怡）希望在年轻女性群体中强化品牌认知，重塑"闺蜜小聚专属饮品"的场景定位。品牌亟需通过节日节点活动与线上互动机制，打造差异化的情绪连接与参与感。

In the context of China's booming low-alcohol "self-pleasure" market, Horoyoi aimed to reinforce its position as the drink of choice for female bonding occasions. The brand sought seasonal campaigns and interactive engagement to strengthen emotional relevance.

项目目标 / Objectives

🎯

突出"姐妹小聚的专属果酒"场景定位

Establish Horoyoi as the go-to drink for intimate female gatherings

💫

提升品牌的情绪记忆与节日参与度

Enhance seasonal brand memory and participation

✨

打造全年可延续的"怡式感"品牌体系

Build a year-round thematic experience of "Yishi-style"

我的贡献 / My Role

品牌洞察与分析 / Consumer Insight

  • 分析"颜值经济""悦己消费"等趋势与社交行为 Analysis of "appearance economy" and "self-pleasure consumption" trends
  • 梳理品牌与竞品在"独饮 vs 聚饮"场景的差异 Mapping brand differentiation in solo vs. social drinking scenarios
  • 确立"怡式感"（精致、情绪、分享）的品牌主张 Establishing the "Yishi-style" brand proposition

创意协助与内容支持 / Creative & Content Contribution

  • 参与构思全年节日节点活动（如樱花野餐、美甲派样、温泉联名） Co-creating seasonal campaign concepts
  • 起草部分传播文案、活动机制逻辑与话术建议 Drafting campaign copy and engagement mechanics
  • 参与用户传播路径及社群组团机制的策划 Planning user journey and community engagement

传播机制与用户参与设计 / Mechanism Design

  • 协助设计"3罐成团，赢春日野餐体验"组队机制 Designing group purchase mechanics
  • 参与 AR 扫码互动、美甲贴纸派样、社交转发流程设计 Developing AR interaction and social sharing process
  • 结合抖音、小红书平台传播节奏，提升转发率与用户UGC参与度 Optimizing UGC engagement across social platforms

该项目属于奥美广告公司服务客户的内部提案，部分视觉内容与客户素材受保密协议限制，无法对外展示。当前仅保留策划结构与内容说明，相关资料可于面试中线下展示。

Due to NDA restrictions as part of Ogilvy's internal proposal, visual assets and proprietary materials are not publicly available. Structural overviews and contribution summaries are shown; full content may be presented privately.

项目背景 / Background

该项目由澳大利亚非营利出版社 Corella Press 主导，旨在将华语文学作品引介至英语文化语境。所选作品《Home Making》聚焦移民家庭的日常生活与文化适应，情感表达细腻，语言节奏复杂，对翻译提出较高要求。

Led by Corella Press, this project aimed to bring Chinese-language literary works into the English-speaking world. Home Making explores the emotional nuances of immigrant family life, making it a translation-intensive and culturally layered text.

项目任务 / Task Description

我担任中译英翻译负责人，带领小组完成翻译、统筹与校审工作。该译文现已正式出版，并被澳大利亚文学数据库 AustLit 收录。

As lead translator, I coordinated the full translation, editing, and localization of the short story into English. The final work has been published by Corella Press and archived in AustLit.

我的贡献 / My Role

翻译统筹与语言风格把控 / Translation Management

  • 统筹进度与任务分配，确保按期交稿 Coordinated progress and task allocation
  • 校审译文风格统一性，保持原文叙事情感与节奏 Maintained stylistic consistency and narrative rhythm
  • 调整繁体/港澳语境中的表达方式，优化译文自然度 Optimized expressions for natural flow

跨文化表达与传播适配 / Cultural Adaptation

  • 对移民家庭文化与情感背景进行延伸研究 Conducted research on immigrant family dynamics
  • 采用更地道的英文表达传递华语文化特有语感 Conveyed Chinese cultural nuances in idiomatic English
  • 提供脚注与背景说明，帮助英文读者理解语境 Added contextual notes for English readers

项目背景 / Background

NeoBike 是一场由哈佛商学院案例系统设计的商业模拟，由我们团队代表公司董事会向高管层进行季度提案与资源配置。该项目模拟真实企业在不同生命周期中的战略抉择，从市场进入、品牌管理到渠道策略全流程实践。

NeoBike was a strategic simulation modeled on Harvard Business School's interactive case system. Acting as VP – Brand Manager, I led brand positioning, digital media strategy, and pricing coordination throughout multiple decision rounds.

项目目标 / Objectives

🎯

在"工作+休闲"两个高潜市场中构建品牌领导地位

Achieve leadership in both Work & Recreation segments

💰

在低成本压力下维持品牌形象与利润平衡

Balance pricing power and profitability under cost pressure

📈

优化销售渠道与定价策略，实现可持续增长

Scale sales channels and integrate a coherent brand experience

我的贡献 / My Role

品牌定位与广告策略 / Brand & Advertising Strategy

  • 基于深度消费者调研，确定主打场景为"城市通勤+轻运动" Identified key scenarios: urban commuting & light recreation
  • 撰写广告文案主张"From City Streets to Mountain Peaks" Created "From City Streets to Mountain Peaks" campaign
  • 带领团队实现品牌认知度在全球三大区域保持第一 Achieved #1 brand awareness across three global regions

定价策略与品牌价值维系 / Pricing & Value Management

  • 拒绝单纯成本导向定价，主张"竞争+价值导向混合定价" Implemented hybrid pricing strategy beyond cost-based approach
  • 实现定价动态调整：初期高价建立价值感，后期配合$100 rebate提高转化 Executed dynamic pricing with strategic rebate program
  • 与团队协作优化毛利结构，实现单位成本下降21.5% Achieved 21.5% unit cost reduction through optimization

战略调整与风险应对 / Strategic Adjustment

  • 及时识别"过度折扣可能削弱品牌溢价"的风险 Identified risks in excessive discounting strategy
  • 制定后期"削减 rebate、回归产品价值"的策略建议 Developed strategy to reduce rebates and restore value
  • 结合 BCG 模型提出产品线上下游拓展方案 Proposed product line expansion using BCG matrix

项目成果 / Key Outcomes

品牌分数在 Q8 达到全区域第一（Work: APAC 93，Recreation: LATAM-MEA 94） Achieved #1 brand score across regions in Q8

销量跃升至领先地位（单品销量达 2,198 台） Reached leading sales position with 2,198 units

促成公司从差异化策略向"成本领先+价值混合"成功转型 Successfully transformed strategy to cost leadership + value hybrid

All content is part of an academic business simulation and can be displayed publicly. Supporting visuals including brand score trajectories, channel strategy maps, and pricing matrices are available on request.

项目背景 / Background

疫情期间，大量中国留学生被迫长期滞留澳洲，情绪焦虑、孤独感上升。我洞察到这一群体对"有生命陪伴"的真实需求，于是创立 Creamy Cattery ——一个专为留学生群体服务的布偶猫品牌，提供从领养、照护到售后的一体化支持服务。

During the pandemic, many Chinese international students were stranded in Australia, facing isolation and emotional stress. I identified a strong unmet need for companionship and founded Creamy Cattery to provide reliable, affordable, and emotionally supportive pet ownership experiences.

项目目标 / Objectives

🤝

响应疫情下的情感陪伴需求

Respond to emotional needs during COVID

💰

提供经济、信赖、有保障的布偶猫领养选择

Offer safe and affordable adoption options

📈

通过服务链路拓展实现资金正循环

Build self-sustaining cash flow

我的贡献 / My Role

疫情下的用户洞察 / User Insight

  • 分析留学生论坛、微信群、豆瓣等平台情绪走向 Analyzed student forums and social media sentiment
  • 访谈潜在用户了解陪伴型宠物需求与顾虑 Conducted user interviews about pet companionship needs
  • 明确布偶猫"高颜值+性格温顺"的核心价值点 Identified core value proposition of Ragdolls

服务产品设计与资源整合 / Service Design

  • 开发"一猫一档"成长记录服务，增强客户信任感 Developed individual pet growth tracking system
  • 制定疫苗接种、绝育、芯片植入等配套服务流程 Established comprehensive pet care procedures
  • 拓展猫舍周边服务（如宠物接送、跨州运输） Expanded to pet transport and related services

盈利机制探索 / Business Model

  • 通过线下服务获得现金流：接送宠物、跨州运输 Generated cash flow through offline services
  • 与兽医、运输商协作，提供宠物跨州运输服务 Partnered with vets and transport providers
  • 逐步将服务模式标准化并可复用于猫舍客户 Standardized service model for scalability

用户增长与传播 / Growth Strategy

  • 利用真实领养故事+照片，在小红书/朋友圈展开私域种草 Leveraged authentic adoption stories for marketing
  • 搭建微信用户社群，持续提供照护建议，提升粘性 Built WeChat community for ongoing engagement
  • 完善售后回访与"陪伴反馈"机制，提高转介绍率 Enhanced follow-up system for referrals

This is an independently founded and operated venture. Supporting materials—such as service flowcharts, pet diaries, customer testimonials, and early-stage financial summaries—are available for portfolio display.
